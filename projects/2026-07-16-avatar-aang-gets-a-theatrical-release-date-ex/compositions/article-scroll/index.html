'Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender' to Play in Select Theaters for Awards Qualifying Run

For one week, "Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender" will screen in select movie theaters in Los Angeles and New York, Variety has learned exclusively.

While the film is primarily a streaming release and will have its Paramount+ debut on July 25, it has also been set for an awards-qualifying theatrical run from July 24 to 30, with three showtimes per day at the AMC Burbank Town Center 6 in L.A. and the AMC Empire 25 in Manhattan.

How Breaching the 45-Day Exclusive Window Will Devastate Movies

Over the past half-century, the exclusive theatrical window has shrunk from a six-month standard in the 1980s and 1990s to 90 days in the post-millennial period. It then settled into the post-pandemic range of 60 to 45 days for most studio films.

This is not hyperbole; it is a fact supported by data and disciplined analysis.
